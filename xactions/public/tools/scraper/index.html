Post Scraper

A clean, monochrome panel that drops onto any feed you're viewing and harvests posts straight from the DOM — X / Twitter first-class, generic post lists as a fallback. No API keys, no servers, no data leaving your browser. Set how many you want, filter by keyword, pause and resume at will, then export.

Launch it

javascript:(function(){var s=document.createElement('script');s.src='https://raw.githack.com/nirholas/XActions/main/public/tools/post-scraper.js?'+Date.now();document.body.appendChild(s);})();

Open the feed you want to scrape, open DevTools → Console, paste the line above, and hit Enter. The panel appears top-right. Run it again to hide it. (Swap the URL for wherever you host XActions — or paste the contents of post-scraper.js directly.)

What it does

Set a targetScrape an exact count, or leave it blank to run until the feed ends.
Keyword filtersLook-for and avoid lists, ANY/ALL matching, case-sensitive and regex modes.
Pause · Resume · StopFull control mid-run; the count you've gathered is always held.
Scroll pacingTune the delay between scrolls, add jitter, and auto-stop at the feed's end.
Export anywhereJSON, NDJSON, CSV, Markdown or plain text — download or copy to clipboard.
Smart captureOn X it grabs author, time, likes, reposts, replies, views, media & permalink.
De-dup & refineDrop duplicates, replies, reposts, or keep media-only and min-likes posts.
Yours to keepSettings persist locally. Draggable, collapsible, monochrome by design.

Everything runs locally in your browser against content already loaded on the page — nothing is sent anywhere. Respect X's Terms of Service and only collect what you're permitted to. You're responsible for how you use what you export.

How to use it

  1. Open the feed and launch the panel.
  2. Enter how many posts you want (or leave blank for the whole feed).
  3. Add look-for / avoid keywords, comma-separated.
  4. Open Advanced to set scroll delay, author filters, min-likes, and skip rules.
  5. Pick an export format, hit Start, and watch the live counter.
  6. Pause any time; Export or Copy when you're done.
