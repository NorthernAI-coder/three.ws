2026-05-21 Listing x402 Coinbase

three.ws Listed on Coinbase x402 Bazaar and agentic.market

three.ws is now a verified service provider on the Coinbase x402 Bazaar and agentic.market — the app store and discovery layer for AI agents. The Bazaar currently serves 480,000 active agents, 100,000 services, $50M in volume, and 165 million transactions, with OpenAI and AWS among its partners.

What the x402 Bazaar is

The Coinbase x402 Bazaar is the canonical discovery surface for AI agent services that accept machine-to-machine payments via the x402 protocol. It's where agents, developers, and LLM clients find paid services they can call with on-chain USDC, without any API key signup, billing account, or manual configuration.

agentic.market is the consumer-facing layer on top — effectively the App Store interface for agent services that the Bazaar powers. A listing on both means three.ws is findable by both technical clients crawling the registry and end users browsing the marketplace.

What being listed means for three.ws

three.ws services listed on the Bazaar are callable by any x402-compatible agent or AI client. That means:

  • Claude, GPT, and other AI assistants can discover and pay for three.ws 3D agent capabilities without any developer setup — they find the service through the Bazaar, send USDC via x402, and get the result.
  • Developers building agent pipelines can wire three.ws into their agent workflows as a paid capability — avatar creation, on-chain identity, animation control — billed per call in USDC.
  • Agent-to-agent transactions are enabled. One three.ws agent can pay another to perform work on its behalf, with the payment settled on-chain and the Bazaar providing the discovery layer.

x402 infrastructure compounds

The Bazaar listing is part of a broader positioning around x402 infrastructure. HTTP 402 — "Payment Required" — has been in the HTTP specification since 1997. x402 activates it on-chain. Every API that accepts x402 payment is part of an emerging machine-economy where AI agents can transact without human wallets or developer-mediated billing.

three.ws is one of the earliest 3D agent platforms to be natively x402-compatible across the entire stack: the MCP server, the <agent-3d> component, and now the Bazaar listing all speak the same payment language.
