three.ws Listed on BNB Chain's Dappbay Directory

May 17, 2026 · three.ws

three.ws is now listed on Dappbay, BNB Chain's official dApp directory. The listing is live under the categories AI Agent Launchpad, AI Data, and AI Infra, with a current rank of AI #121.

  • Dappbay listing: dappbay.bnbchain.org/detail/three
  • Open source: github.com/nirholas/three.ws
  • Project site: three.ws

What three.ws ships

three.ws is the platform for 3D AI agents on-chain. Every <agent-3d> tag deploys an autonomous 3D character with:

  • A Solana NFT identity. Each agent is minted as a transferable on-chain asset.
  • An ERC-8004 wallet. The emerging cross-chain agent-identity standard, baked in.
  • A browser-native 3D body. Rendered in WebGL — no plugins, no native install.
  • Native x402 / HTTP 402 payments. Creators monetize through time-passes, trials, and per-session payments in USDC, with earnings settled on-chain.

Why Dappbay matters

Dappbay is BNB Chain's official discovery layer for decentralized applications — the dApp directory that BNB Chain itself publishes and that the wider BSC ecosystem uses to find, vet, and rank projects. A Dappbay listing puts three.ws inside that discovery flow and surfaces it to the BNB Chain community looking for AI agent infrastructure, launchpads, and tooling.

three.ws settles agent payments on Solana, Base, and Polygon today; the Dappbay presence is about distribution and reach into BNB Chain's AI dApp audience, not a chain migration. The underlying x402 protocol is chain-agnostic, and we're open to expanding payment rails wherever agent demand lives.
