# F06 — "Why three.ws" positioning page

**Track:** Advertise & Value · **Size:** S/M · **Priority:** P3

## Goal
A clear positioning page that states what three.ws is best at and why someone would choose it —
honestly framed against the alternatives a user would otherwise consider (generic avatar makers,
chatbot builders, NFT launchers).

## Why it matters
Prospects evaluate options. A confident, honest positioning page that names the unique combination
(3D avatar + AI brain + embed + optional on-chain ownership/payments, all in one) helps them
decide — and arms partners and press with the narrative.

## Context
- The unique value is the *integration* of capabilities the audit listed as currently feeling disconnected. This page makes the integration the point.
- Honesty rule: comparisons must be fair and accurate; don't disparage or misrepresent competitors. Focus on what three.ws uniquely combines.

## Scope
- A positioning page: the core thesis, who it's for, the unique combination, and honest "use three.ws when… / it's overkill when…" guidance.
- An optional, fair comparison (capability matrix) — accurate, not a hit piece.
- Strong CTA; Track B styling; link from nav/footer and the homepage.

## Definition of done
- A visitor can read the page and understand precisely what makes three.ws different and whether it fits their need; claims are accurate and fair.

## Verify
- Fact-check every claim against real platform capabilities; confirm the page links from nav/footer and reads as confident, not hype.
