# G02 — Demo video scripts + storyboards (record-ready)

**Track:** Partnerships & Content · **Size:** M · **Priority:** P2 · **Pairs with:** F01

## Goal
Record-ready scripts and shot-by-shot storyboards for short demo videos of the flagship features
and partner integrations — so the founder (or an editor) can capture polished content quickly.

## Why it matters
You want videos and content for partners. The bottleneck is usually scripting/planning, not
recording. Tight scripts + storyboards that map to real, working flows make production fast and
consistent.

## Context
- Flagship flows to script: selfie→3D scan, text→3D forge, AR walk, build-an-agent + embed, a coin launch with the `3ws` mark, and the IBM/partner demos.
- Demos must show **real** product behavior (honesty rule). The flows exist today — script against what's actually on screen.

## Scope
- For each flagship: a short script (hook → value → demo beats → CTA), a storyboard (per-shot: on-screen action, what to click, expected result, b-roll), target length, and the exact route/steps to capture.
- A consistent intro/outro and brand framing ($THREE only, accurate partner language).
- Deliver as `docs/video-scripts/<feature>.md`; keep them in sync with the real flows.

## Definition of done
- Each flagship has a record-ready script + storyboard mapping to the real flow, with a clear CTA and consistent branding.

## Verify
- Walk one storyboard against the live product — every beat matches what's actually on screen.
