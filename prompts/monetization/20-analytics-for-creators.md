# Prompt 20: Analytics for Creators

## Objective
Provide creators with analytics about how their skills are performing in the marketplace.

## Explanation
Data-driven insights can help creators optimize their offerings and pricing.

## Instructions
1.  **Track Metrics:**
    *   Start tracking key metrics, such as skill page views, clicks on the "Purchase" button, and successful purchases.

2.  **Create an Analytics Dashboard:**
    *   In the creator dashboard, add a new section for analytics.
    *   Display charts and graphs for:
        *   Sales over time.
        *   Conversion rate (views to sales).
        *   Top-performing skills.
        *   Revenue by skill.
