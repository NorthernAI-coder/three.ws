How it works

From a blank page to a walking avatar in three steps.

  1. 1

    Create your avatar

    Generate one from a selfie or a text prompt in Forge — or use any humanoid GLB. It comes back fully rigged and animation-ready.

  2. 2

    Walk on three.ws

    Open the Walk app and drive your avatar with WASD or a touch joystick. Drag to orbit the camera, sprint to run — real locomotion, not a turntable.

  3. 3

    Embed it anywhere

    Copy one iframe tag onto any site, or install the Chrome extension so your avatar follows you across the entire web.

One tag. Any site.

No SDK, no build step. Paste the iframe, set the avatar and world with query params, and you have a live walking avatar.

your-site.html 
<!-- three.ws Walk — drop in anywhere -->
<iframe
  src="https://three.ws/walk-embed?env=void&autoplay=true"
  width="360" height="480"
  style="border:0;background:transparent"
  title="three.ws walking avatar"></iframe>
