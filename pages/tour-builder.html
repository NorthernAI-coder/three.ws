Ship it to Shopify

Three copy-pastes and your store has a walking 3D guide. Everything below is generated from the tour you just built.

1 Upload your tour script

In Shopify admin → Content → Files, upload this file, then copy the URL Shopify gives it.

2 Add the guide to your theme

Online Store → Themes → ⋯ → Edit code → layout/theme.liquid. Paste this right before </body>, and swap the URL for the one from step 1.

3 Add a "Take the tour" button

Drop this anywhere in your theme — a section, a Custom Liquid block, your header. Any element with data-tour-start becomes a start button.
