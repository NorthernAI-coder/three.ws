View in AR

Every avatar and Forge model has a View in AR button. On mobile it anchors the model to real surfaces through the camera. On desktop a QR code lets you scan and place in one tap. No app needed.

For: anyone who wants to see their 3D agent at real-world scale — on their desk, floor, or shelf.

Why it's better: Three AR methods in one button — iOS Quick Look, Android Scene Viewer, and WebXR. The right one fires automatically. The agent stays live in WebXR, so conversation and animations work in AR.
