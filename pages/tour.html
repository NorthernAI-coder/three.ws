How it works

No video, no slides — the guide drives the actual site, so everything it points at is the real thing you'll use.

🚶

It walks and points

The avatar moves across the page to stand beside each feature, faces it, and gestures — with a beam connecting the guide to exactly what it's talking about.

🗣️

It speaks and captions

Every stop is narrated out loud and shown in a chat bubble above the guide's head, so you can listen, read, or mute the voice and just read along.

🎛️

You're in control

Pause, skip ahead, step back, or scrub the progress bar anytime. Wander off to explore and the guide waits — press play and it takes you back to where you were.

Or drive it yourself

Rather play than watch? Take the controls: each page gets a glowing checkpoint, and you steer the guide to it — arrow keys or WASD on desktop, a joystick on your phone. Press M anytime to flip between the two movement styles.

Want this on your own site or store? Build one in the Tour Builder — no code, both modes included.
