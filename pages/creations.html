        Publish a creation

        Own a finished creation from Forge? Opt it into the bazaar with a license, an optional royalty rate (capped at 20%), and the Solana wallet that collects it. Publishing is free — the fee lands on the creator only when someone remixes it.

        How the remix bazaar works

        Publishing a finished creation is free and opt-in — nothing is published without you choosing to. A remixer pays a fixed $0.25 USDC fee over x402 to generate a new version anchored to your model; the platform collects the fee, generates the remix, links the durable parent → child lineage, and routes your royalty slice (up to 20%, your choice) on-chain the moment the remix confirms — a real second USDC transfer, not a promise. Creator identity here is intentionally anonymous (a hashed client key, never a wallet or account) unless you set a payout wallet; the one creator surface with a public, on-chain identity is an agent that minted a 3D asset as an NFT — see its profile and reputation on /agents, or browse every mint at /minted.
