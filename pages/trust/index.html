Three layers, one governed turn

Nothing here is staged. The brain and both guardrails are real calls to IBM watsonx.ai; the audit trail is real cryptography in your browser.

01 · BRAIN

Thinks on IBM Granite

The agent's replies are generated by ibm/granite-3-8b-instruct through watsonx.ai's /ml/v1/text/chat endpoint, scoped to your own IBM Cloud project.

02 · GUARDRAILS

Screened by Granite Guardian

Before and after every reply, granite-guardian-3-8b classifies the turn for harm, social bias, jailbreak, violence and profanity — returning a verdict and confidence per risk.

03 · ATTESTATION

Sealed & anchorable

Each governed turn becomes a SHA-256 receipt, hash-chained to the last and Ed25519-signed. Edit any past turn and verification fails. Anchor the head on Solana for an immutable timestamp.
