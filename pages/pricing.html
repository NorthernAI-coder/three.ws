FAQ

What’s included in the free beta?

Everything: unlimited avatars, unlimited agents, unlimited embeds, voice, animation, mocap, AR, x402 paid skills, the dashboard, and the API. We’re intentionally not metering anything during beta — we want to see how people actually use the platform before drawing pricing lines.

What happens when beta ends?

A generous free tier will stay free forever for hobby and open-source projects. Paid tiers will be usage-based on embed views, chat turns, and API calls — priced to be cheaper than rolling your own. Existing users get 60 days’ notice and a grandfathered transition rate.

Can I monetize my own agents?

Yes. The x402 protocol lets you charge per skill invocation (per chat turn, per generation, per API call) in USDC on Solana. During beta you keep 100% of revenue. After beta there will be a small platform fee (target: 2.9%, similar to Stripe).

Do I need crypto to use three.ws?

No. The web component, dashboard, and API work without a wallet. Crypto unlocks: on-chain agent identity (ERC-8004), payable skills (x402), and creator payouts. If you don’t need those, the platform works like any other SaaS.

What about enterprise pricing?

Custom subdomain, SSO, audit logs, embed-policy whitelisting, white-label themes, and SLA. Email hello@three.ws and we’ll set up a call.
