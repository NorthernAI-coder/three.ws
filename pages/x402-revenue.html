How this works

Each paid endpoint settles a small USDC payment per call. Every settlement is recorded in the revenue ledger and surfaced here — never a simulation. See x402 revenue & receipts and the endpoint catalog.

Operational fields (IP, user agent) are never exposed — only the on-chain-public route, amount, payer, and transaction. Amounts shown in USDC.
