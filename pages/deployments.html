About this feed

ERC-8004 is the open standard for portable on-chain agent identity. Every agent here is registered on an Identity Registry deployed at the same address on each chain. Deploy yours from the studio or explore the 3D agent directory.

Network: mainnet. Source: erc8004_agents_index, crawled from chain logs. Registrations without a confirmed block time are counted in totals but not placed in the timeline.
