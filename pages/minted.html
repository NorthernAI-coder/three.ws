How minting works here

Minting is always opt-in — the free text→3D lane never auto-mints. A creator picks a generated or owned GLB, an optional royalty rate (capped at 10%), and mints it on devnet (default, free testing) or mainnet (a real, disclosed on-chain write). Every mint bakes provenance — creator, prompt, generation model, and parent lineage — into both the NFT metadata and a second, independently-signed record on the platform's append-only provenance ledger. Naming another creator's mint as a parent_mint (a remix) routes their royalty share out of your mint fee as a real on-chain USDC transfer, the moment your mint confirms. Read more in the MCP tool docs or query any mint back with get_3d_asset_onchain.
