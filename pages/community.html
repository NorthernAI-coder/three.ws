Ways to get involved

  • Ship an agent. Create one at /create, embed it on your site, and share it on X with #threews.
  • Report bugs. Open an issue on GitHub — we triage daily.
  • Contribute code. PRs are welcome. Check the repo for open issues labeled good first issue.
  • Build an integration. The MCP server, A2A protocol, and x402 payment layer are all open for third-party tools.
  • Share feedback. Tweet at us, open a discussion on GitHub, or email hello@three.ws.
