How it works

Frames stream through a feed-forward transformer that grounds coordinates, reads dense geometry, and corrects drift across the whole clip — no per-scene optimisation. The result is a world-space coloured point cloud. Output renders with WebGL THREE.Points; this is a point cloud, not a Gaussian splat.
