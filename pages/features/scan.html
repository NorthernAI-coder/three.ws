How it works

Camera in, 3D avatar out

01

Point your camera

Open the scanner on any phone or desktop browser with camera access. Position your face in the guide frame — good light, neutral expression, camera at eye level.

02

Multi-angle capture

The scanner captures several angles automatically and sends them to our reconstruction pipeline, which builds a full 3D mesh with UV-mapped skin texture.

03

Rigged GLB, ready to use

The output is a rigged GLB — the skeleton is wired so standard BVH or mixamo animations work out of the box. Download it, embed it, or launch your agent with it.

What you get
