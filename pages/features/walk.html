How it works

Three steps to your avatar in motion

01

Choose your avatar

Pick any avatar from your library, browse the gallery, or paste a GLB URL directly. Share a walk link with ?avatar= to deep-link others into the same avatar.

02

Move with WASD or joystick

Desktop: WASD or arrow keys to move, mouse to rotate camera. Mobile: on-screen joystick and swipe. The avatar uses real physics — it walks, runs, and turns naturally.

03

Toggle AR for real-world placement

Tap the AR button on iOS or Android to enter WebXR passthrough. The avatar detects your floor and walks on it. Point your phone at any surface and it adapts.

Built for real-world use

◉

Real physics

Powered by Rapier physics — kinematic character controller, heightfield collision, and smooth step-up so the avatar navigates terrain naturally.

◉

WebXR AR passthrough

One tap switches to camera passthrough on supported devices. The avatar places itself on your detected floor and walks through your real environment.

◉

Shareable link

Pass ?avatar=<id>&handle=<handle> in the URL to generate a shareable OG card that shows the avatar thumbnail when unfurled in Slack, X, or iMessage.

◉

Embeddable

The walk experience can be embedded as a fullscreen iframe on any website — use it as a live avatar demo on your landing page or product docs.
