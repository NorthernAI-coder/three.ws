How it works

From mint to world in seconds

01

Pick a coin

Search by name or paste any pump.fun mint address. The $THREE world is always available as the home town — enter it without a mint.

02

Land in the world

Your avatar spawns in a procedurally generated terrain seeded by that coin's mint. Every coin maps to one of six biomes — the world is always the same for any given mint.

03

Walk, chat, and trade

Use WASD or joystick to move. Chat with other holders in the live text channel. The trade panel lets you buy or sell the coin without leaving the world.

What makes it different

◈

Deterministic worlds

A coin's mint hash deterministically picks its biome and seeds its terrain — the world is always consistent, not random per-visit.

◈

Real holders, live

Other wallets that hold the coin can walk the same world at the same time — real multiplayer with real community context.

◈

Trade in-world

The buy/sell panel is always one click away while you explore — no tab-switching, no leaving the experience.

◈

Fishing + mini-games

Each world has activities like fishing for in-world items. The economy layer grows with each new world slice we ship.
