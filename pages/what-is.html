In one sentence

three.ws lets anyone turn a selfie or a description into a 3D AI agent — a character you can talk to, animate, and embed on any website with two lines of code. No 3D modelling, no game engine, and no crypto required to start.

One platform, not seven tools

The avatar creator, the animation, the AI chat, the embed widget, and the worlds are all building blocks for the same agent. Make it once — use it everywhere.
