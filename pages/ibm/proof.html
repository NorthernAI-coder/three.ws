Granite Proof.
Auditable AI, on a public ledger.

A 3D agent forecasts a live crypto market with IBM Granite TimeSeries. IBM Granite Guardian governs every word it says. Then it notarizes the governed forecast on Solana — a SHA-256 proof of exactly what the model predicted, signed by the agent's own wallet, timestamped forever. If Guardian flags the statement, the agent refuses to sign.
