Agent wallets

The wallets that move money for you. Authorize agent wallets to auto-pay for x402 calls (with per-payment and daily caps), and set payout wallets that receive your revenue. Caps are enforced before any signature.

Security & CORS

Control who can call your paid endpoints, how much an agent can spend, and which origins may embed your buttons.

Spending caps

Hard limits applied to agent-wallet auto-payments before any transaction is signed.

When on, returning buyers prove wallet ownership (CAIP-122) before the modal will charge — blocks anonymous drains.

CORS allowlist

Origins permitted to embed your pay buttons and call your checkout. Empty = same-origin only.

API bypass key

A secret your subscribers send (as Authorization: Bearer …) to skip payment on your endpoints. Shown once on rotation — store it safely.
